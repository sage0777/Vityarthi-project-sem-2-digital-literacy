# **Stay Safe Online Checklist (For College Students in India)**



## ***Prevention Tips***



* ##### Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable 2FA wherever possible.
* ##### Never share OTPs, UPI PINs, or banking details with anyone.
* ##### Do not approve unknown UPI collect requests or scan QR codes sent by strangers.
* ##### Always verify payment requests through a trusted contact before sending money.
* ##### Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
* ##### Keep your apps and phone updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.
* ##### Do not click on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or social media messages.
* ##### Limit personal information shared on social media (phone number, address, etc.).z
* ##### Install apps only from official stores like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
* ##### Regularly check bank statements and transaction history for unauthorized activity.

## ***Reflection (100–150 words)***



##### While researching UPI online payment fraud, what surprised me most was how easily fraudsters manipulate people using urgency and trust. Even educated individuals, including college students, fall victim due to simple mistakes like sharing OTPs or approving unknown payment requests. The speed at which money gets transferred and the difficulty in recovering it was also alarming.

##### As a result, one habit I will personally change is being more cautious before making any online transaction. I will always verify requests, especially those involving money, even if they appear to come from known contacts. Additionally, I will regularly monitor my bank transactions and avoid clicking on unknown links or downloading unverified apps.
