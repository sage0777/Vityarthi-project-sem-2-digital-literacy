* ### **Do’s**



##### 1\. Think before you post – ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.

##### 2\. Maintain a professional image – future employers may view your profile.

##### 3\. Protect your privacy – use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings.

##### 4\. Verify information before sharing – avoid spreading misinformation.

##### 5\. Be respectful in interactions – engage politely in comments and discussions.



* ### **Don’ts**



##### 1\. Don’t share personal/sensitive information (address, passwords, etc.).

##### 2\. Don’t post offensive or harmful content (hate speech, bullying).

##### 3\. Don’t overshare – avoid posting every detail of your life publicly.

##### 4\. Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling.

##### 5\. Don’t ignore digital footprint – remember posts can stay online permanently.
